Revolt Against the Modern World

"Revolt Against the Modern World" by Julius Evola is a highly influential work in the tradition of traditionalist thought. Evola offers a critique of modernity and proposes a return to the spiritual values and traditions of the past. While some of his ideas may be controversial and challenging to modern sensibilities, the book is an important contribution to the ongoing conversation about the meaning of tradition and the challenges facing contemporary society.
