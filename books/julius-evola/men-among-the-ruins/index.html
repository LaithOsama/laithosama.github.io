Men Among the Ruins

"Men Among the Ruins" by Julius Evola is a powerful and insightful book that critiques the modern world and offers a vision for a new spiritual order. Evola's analysis of the crisis of modernity is particularly relevant today, as we face many of the same problems that he wrote about nearly a century ago. His call for a return to traditional values and a rejection of the materialistic and individualistic mindset that dominates our society is one that we should take seriously if we hope to build a more meaningful and fulfilling existence.
