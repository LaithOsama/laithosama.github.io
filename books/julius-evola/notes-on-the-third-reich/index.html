Notes on the Third Reich

"Notes on the Third Reich" by Julius Evola is a controversial book that has been both praised and condemned for its exploration of the relationship between Traditionalist philosophy and the National Socialist movement. Evola was a complex figure who held a wide range of beliefs and opinions, and his writings on Nazi Germany reflect his idiosyncratic perspective. While some readers may find his ideas thought-provoking and insightful, others may reject them as misguided or even dangerous.
