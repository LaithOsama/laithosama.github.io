Pagan Imperialism and the Metaphysics of War

: Julius Evola's "Pagan Imperialism and the Metaphysics of War" is a thought-provoking book that challenges traditional ideas of imperialism and offers a unique perspective on the role of spirituality in warfare. While I do not endorse all of Evola's ideas, I believe that this book offers valuable insights into the connections between politics, religion, and war.
