Ride the Tiger

This book by Julius Evola is a masterpiece that challenges the values of modern society and calls for a return to traditional philosophy. The author argues that the modern world is in a state of decline and encourages readers to embrace their individuality and connect with their inner spirituality. The book is well-researched and thought-provoking, and it has the potential to inspire readers to live a more meaningful life. In short, "Ride the Tiger" is a thought-provoking and inspiring book that I highly recommend to anyone seeking a deeper understanding of the world and their place in it.
